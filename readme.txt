    == Description ==
    POWr Photo Gallery is a mobile-responsive, fully-customizable plugin that you can edit **right in the page**.
    Add the widget to your site, or use the shortcode **[powr-photo-gallery]** to add Photo Gallery to any post.
    <br><br>
    Display your photos and images with a stunning Pinterest-style image gallery. The layout is algorithmically generate to beautifully display any combination of images. You can also add titles, text, videos, buttons and more. Choose from expert-designed templates, or completely customize the look and feel of your image gallery, including the layout, fonts, and colors.<br><br>Customize the layout and spacing - from multi-columns to serve as a centerpiece of a page, or a single column on a side panel. Add unlimited images, and join them with any combination of text, videos, buttons, links and more. Automatic share links for Facebook, Twitter, and many other common social networks. Link to any page or website.  <br><br>The fonts, colors, and backgrounds are also fully customizable. Add subtle borders and shadows to breathe life into your photos.
    <br><br>
    The basic version of the POWr Photo Gallery is free!
    <br><br>
    POWr plugins can be used an virtually any website, learn more at [www.POWr.io](http://www.powr.io/)
    <br><br>
    Features:
    <br><br>
    * Add unlimited photos<br> * Layout algorithmically generated to beautifully display any combination of images<br> * Add titles, text, videos, buttons, links, and more<br> * Customize column width and spacing<br> * Automatically generated links to share on facebook, twitter, and more<br> * Link photos to any page or website<br> * Customize all fonts, colors, and backgrounds to fit your brand<br> * Language support - customize text to use any language<br> * Mobile responsive - designed to look great on desktop, tablets, and mobile


    == Installation ==
    To install POWr Photo Gallery
    <br><br>
    1. Upload 'powr-photo-gallery.php' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
    <br>
    2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
    <br><br>
    To add POWr Photo Gallery as a widget, go to **Appearance->Widgets**, and drag POWr Photo Gallery to where you'd like it to appear.
    <br><br>
    To add Photo Gallery within a post or a page, simply write the shortcode **[powr-photo-gallery label='MY LABEL']** (including the brackets) where you'd like your Photo Gallery to appear. Then visit your page!
    <br><br>
    Want a more detailed tutorial? Visit [How to add Photo Gallery to your Wordpress site](http://www.powr.io/tutorials/how-to-add-photo-gallery-plugin-to-your-wordpress-site)


    == Frequently Asked Questions ==
    = How can I add Photo Gallery to a blog post or page? = Just add the shortcode **[powr-photo-gallery label='MY LABEL']** (including the brackets) in your post where you'd like your Photo Gallery to appear. Then visit your page!

    = How can I add  Photo Gallery to my site multiple times? = Just be sure to specify different label text, eg **[powr-photo-gallery label='MY LABEL 1']**, **[powr-photo-gallery label='MY LABEL 2']** for each time you use the plugin

    = Why do you require me to sign up? = Signing up is what allows us to track which plugins are yours, and allows you to edit them right in your page.

    = Will my users see the settings icon in the corner of the Photo Gallery? = No! You only see the settings icon because you own the plugins. To temporarily hide it, click on your page and then type 'p + down arrow' (the letter p followed by the down arrow). You can show the icon again by typing 'p + up-arrow'.

    = I can't see the settings icon anymore, what happened? = You are just logged out of POWr.io. You can show the icon again by typing 'p + up-arrow'.

    = Is POWr Photo Gallery Free? = Yes, the basic version of Photo Gallery is completely free!

    = Why should I upgrade? = Upgrading removes all POWr watermarks, speeds up widget loading speed, gets you premium support, and most importantly supports the creation of more great plugins!
